# AI Rules

AI in Rekkus should improve leverage, not become the product.

## Principles

- Deterministic systems first.
- Heuristics and existing data before LLM calls.
- No fake AI magic.
- No AI-first UX unless it clearly improves food discovery.
- No expensive embeddings or vector systems without explicit approval.
- Keep AI work async, bounded, observable, and cost-aware.
- Prefer low-cost models and structured outputs when AI is eventually useful.

## Good Uses Later

- Moderation assistance
- Duplicate restaurant detection
- Cuisine/dish tagging suggestions
- Review summarization
- Metadata cleanup
- Operational summaries

## Deprioritized

- AI-generated reviews
- Full AI concierge/chatbot
- AI-generated feeds
- Heavy conversational systems
- Autonomous recommendation agents

## Rule of Thumb

If Rekkus can solve the problem with product data, rules, SQL, caching, or a small service, do that before AI.
