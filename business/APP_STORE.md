# App Store Strategy

Purpose: capture App Store and Play Store positioning, metadata, ratings, and release presence.

## Current Direction

- Keep metadata aligned with dish-first food discovery.
- Use beta feedback and reviews to refine positioning.
- Track ratings and reviews as an operational input after launch.

## Metadata Strategy

- Lead with "dish-first local food discovery" and saved food intent.
- Screenshots should show Search, saved places/collections, restaurant dish evidence, and profile taste summary before generic feed imagery.
- Keywords should emphasize dishes, local food, saved places, food recommendations, and collections rather than generic restaurant reviews.
- Store copy must not imply restaurant directory completeness, health/safety verification, or AI recommendations.

## Ratings Loop

- Prompt only after successful utility moments such as saving a place, creating a useful review, revisiting a saved restaurant, or sharing a collection.
- Respect cooldowns and do not prompt after errors, auth friction, reports, blocks, or failed payments.
- Track aggregate prompt impressions and outcomes; do not store review text unless entered through the store provider.
- Keep the ratings prompt removable through release configuration until beta evidence is positive.
