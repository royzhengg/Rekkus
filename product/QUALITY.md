# Quality

Owner: Product

Quality means Rekkus content helps someone make a better food decision.

## Quality Dimensions

| Dimension | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Specificity | Names dishes, restaurant context, ratings, or useful details. |
| Recency | Reflects current restaurant and dish reality. |
| Local relevance | Helps nearby discovery and revisit intent. |
| Trust | Comes from real users and safe contribution paths. |
| Density | Adds enough evidence to improve search and restaurant pages. |

## Current Quality Inputs

- Food, vibe, and cost ratings.
- Best-dish field.
- Dish tags on photos.
- Comments, reactions, likes, and saves.
- Restaurant-level aggregates.

## Guardrails

- Do not optimize only for volume.
- Keep low-quality content from dominating discovery.
- Use deterministic quality signals before complex AI or ranking systems.
