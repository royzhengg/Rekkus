# Contribution Loops

Owner: Product

Contribution loops turn useful user actions into better discovery for everyone.

## Contribution Types

- Reviews/posts with ratings and restaurant context.
- Dish tags pinned to photos.
- Best-dish mentions.
- Saves and saved locations.
- Reactions, likes, comments, and follows.

## Quality Rules

- Prompt for dish/place context when it improves discovery.
- Keep contribution steps lightweight and skippable where possible.
- Reward useful specificity over volume.
- Use moderation and trust signals before scaling contribution incentives.

## Signals

- Completed posts with restaurant, cuisine, rating, and dish data.
- Dish tags per post and per restaurant.
- Helpful reactions and saves per contribution.
- Repeat contribution rate after first post.
