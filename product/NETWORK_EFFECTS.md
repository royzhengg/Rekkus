# Network Effects

Owner: Product

Network effects should come from useful taste and dish data compounding across local users, not follower vanity.

## Loops

- More reviews -> denser restaurant and dish evidence -> better discovery.
- More saves -> stronger taste graph -> better recommendations.
- More follows -> better feed relevance -> more high-quality posts.
- More dish tags -> better dish graph -> better long-tail search.

## Current Ingredients

- User profiles and follows.
- Posts with ratings, cuisine, best dish, dish tags, photos, and comments.
- Saves, likes, reactions, and saved locations.
- Search analytics and restaurant interaction signals.

## Guardrails

- Do not prioritize influencer mechanics over decision utility.
- Do not make popularity the only ranking signal.
- Preserve privacy for saved and liked content where product rules require it.
