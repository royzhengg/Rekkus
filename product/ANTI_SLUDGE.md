# Anti-Sludge

Anti-sludge owns product rules that keep Rekkus from drifting into low-utility engagement, dark patterns, or operational mess.

## Sludge To Avoid

- Endless generic feed scrolling that does not help food decisions.
- Vanity ranking, public clout loops, and influencer-first mechanics.
- Forced auth before the user understands value.
- Notifications that exist only to inflate opens.
- Provider data presented as Rekkus trust.
- AI-generated answers that bypass transparent dish/place evidence.
- Paid restaurant influence that looks organic.

## Product Rules

- Every major surface should support a food decision, save, contribution, collection, or revisit.
- Saves matter more than likes.
- Dish/place specificity matters more than vague praise.
- Utility copy beats engagement copy.
- Empty states should guide action without manufacturing urgency.
- Ranking must remain explainable and reversible.

## Review Checklist

Before adding a growth, ranking, AI, notification, or monetization mechanic, ask:

- Does this improve food decision utility?
- Does it preserve trust and user control?
- Can it be rolled back?
- Is the data source clear?
- Would it still be acceptable if shown to beta testers and restaurants?
